New Working hours Rule for Women in U.P.


On 28th May, 2022, the Uttar Pradesh Government has issued new rule for the employment of women workforce in the state to ensure safety of the working women. The circular states that women cannot be forced to work between the hours of 7 pm and 6 am. Consent of that woman employee has to be taken if she is being assigned duty between 7pm and 6 am. This rule is applicable to both government and private sector.

Highlights

  • During night duty, female employees must be provided with food and should have access to drinking water.
  • Companies and Organisations must create a committee to ensure that harassment against women does not take place.
  • Bathrooms, changing rooms must be made available for women in the office.
  • Women employees will only work in the office when there are at least 4 or more female employees on duty as well.


Under the Mission Shakti initiative in the micro and small-scale industries sector, an allocation of Rs 20 crore has been recommended for women's safety, empowerment, and skill development.
