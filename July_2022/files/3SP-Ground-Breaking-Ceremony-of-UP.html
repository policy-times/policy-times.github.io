3rd UP Investors Summit: Highlights


The Groundbreaking Ceremony of the third Uttar Pradesh Investors’ Summit was inaugurated on 3rd June 2022 by Shri Narendra Modi. He laid the foundation stone of 1,406 projects worth over Rs 8,000 crore. The projects were in diverse fields, including agriculture, IT and Electronics, MSME, manufacturing, renewable energy, pharma, tourism, defence and aerospace, handloom, and textile.

Context
 The first Uttar Pradesh Investors Summit was held on July 29, 2018, and the second one took place on July 28 the next year. During the first summit, the foundation stone of 81 projects worth over Rs 61,500 crore was laid. During the second one, the foundation stone of 290 projects with investments of over Rs 67,000 crore was laid.

Highlights

  • Incentives for Manufacturing
    • Gautam Adani, Kumar Mangalam Birla and other leading industrialists are attending the mega summit organised by the Uttar Pradesh government. Defence minister Rajnath Singh and Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath are among those attending the event.
    • 1,406 Projects worth ₹ 80,000 Crore were launched.
    • Adani Group will invest Rs 70,000 crore in Uttar Pradesh.
    • KM Birla Group is investing Rs 40,000 crore projecting the employment of 35000 people.
    • Gautam Buddh Nagar topped the list with highest amount of investment with 238 projects worth Rs 45,529.29 crore.
    • Gautam Buddh Nagar was followed by Lucknow that is due to receive Rs 4,460.88 crore for 45 projects.
    • Gorakhpur was the third followed by Ghaziabad as the fourth position in terms of amount of investment proposal announced.

    Conclusion
    UP being the largest state in population and one of the largest states in terms of area, it’s contribution to the growth and development of India would be significant. UP aims at attracting good number of investments in the state in various potential sectors through these mega summits. The projects announced has the potential to make major transformation in the industrial landscape of the state along with generating the much needed employment opportunities if implemented properly.
