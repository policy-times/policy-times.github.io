Editor's Desk

The Budget for 2022-23 was presented in February. Unsurprisingly, there has been a divergence of opinion on the takeaways. The Government projected an economic growth of 11.1 per cent (which has since been revised downwards) for the next year. The Finance Minister is holding wide post-budget consultations with various stakeholders. The focus on assesses and removing roadblocks has been a key focus. The Government wants to be viewed as assesse friendly.

Trade Deals are back on the table. We signed the India-United Arab Emirates (UAE) Comprehensive Economic Partnership Agreement (CEPA) earlier this month. This was done in double quick time.....

Ministry of Corporate Affairs

Companies (Accounts) Amendment Rules, 2022: New Form CSR 2

Relevant to Central CSR Team, Tech M Foundation and CSR Team of All Mahindra Businesses 

 

  • The Central Government has notified the Companies (Accounts) Amendment Rules, 2022. As per amended rules, every company covered under u/s 135 shall furnish a report on Corporate Social Responsibility in E-Form CSR-2 to the Registrar for the preceding financial year (2020-2021) and onwards.

  • The CSR-2 is to be filed as an addendum to Form AOC-4/AOC-4 XBRL/AOC-4 NBFC (Ind AS) as the case may be. However, form CSR-2 for the F.Y.2020-21 shall be filed separately latest by 31st March 2022.

Ministry of Finance  

Automation in the Customs System

Relevant to M&M - IO

 

  • The Central Board of Indirect Taxes and Customs (CBIC) has issued a circular on 27th February 2022 regarding the implementation of automation in the customs system. The aim of this automation is to make the entire system contact-less and simplify the customs procedure.

  • There are amendments in the Customs (Import of Goods at Concessional Rate of Duty) Rules, 2017 towards automation. These changes come into effect from 1st March 2022.

Ministry of Heavy Industry & Ministry of Road Transport and Highways  

Auto Industry Policy Developments 

Relevant to M&M-Auto & Mahindra Electric

 

  • The Ministry of Heavy Industries has notified a Phased Manufacturing Programme (PMP) to promote indigenous manufacturing of Electric Vehicles, its assemblies / sub-assemblies, and parts / sub-parts / inputs of the sub-assemblies. The PMP envisaged a graded duty structure to promote indigenous manufacturing over a period of time.

  • The Union Minister for Finance and Corporate Affairs, Smt. Nirmala Sitharaman while presenting the Union Budget 2022-23 in Parliament highlighted the constraint of space in urban areas for setting up charging stations at scale and announced the proposal of bringing out Battery Swapping Policy and inter-operability standards.
