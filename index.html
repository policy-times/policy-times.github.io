Editor's Desk

On October 7, 2021, Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi completed 20 successful years in High Public Office. First, as chief minister of Gujarat, and then as Prime Minister.  

As a part of its continuous push for Governance reforms, the Government has decided that from next month, no central government file will pass through more than four hands and ministries will also be able to submit e-files to each other. The idea is to speed up decisionmaking and usher in an executive-centric and business-oriented functional transformation.
The attempt of the Government is to encourage delegation, rather than perpetrate the current process of pushing files up to higher levels endlessly.

Implementation framework - Vehicle Scrappage Policy

Relevant to M&M - Auto and CERO

October 04,2021 

  • In the Vehicle Scrapping Policy, it is proposed to have a system of incentives and disincentives to nudge vehicle owners to discard old and polluting vehicles, which have higher maintenance and fuel consumption costs.

Global Hunger Index 2021 - Key takeaways 

Relevant to CSR Team

October 18, 2021 

  • India plummeted to 101st place out of 116 countries in the Global Hunger Index (GHI) 2021, falling from 94th place in 2020. According to the Global Hunger Index assessment, India's hunger level is "serious," which is an improvement over its performance in 2000, when hunger levels were "alarming."

  • India has slipped to 101st position in the Global Hunger Index (GHI) 2021 of 116 countries, from its 2020 position of 94th. According to the report, India's performance has dropped only because of malnutrition, which the government has addressed.

Canteen services for employees out of GST net 

Relevant to Taxation Team

October 19, 2021
